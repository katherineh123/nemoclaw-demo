💡 Analysis & Recommendation

Germany gains significantly: Transferring 50 workers gets Germany from 67.3% → 82.2% fulfillment at plateau (August onward). It clears the 70% threshold by June 2026 (one month after transfer). The transferred workers enter as new hires and take 3 months to reach full productivity in the new environment.
⚠️ Asia takes a severe hit: Losing 50 tenured workers drops Asia from 65.0% → 47.0% — a catastrophic decline well into 🔴 Critical territory. Asia is already below the 70% target and this would make it drastically worse.
Net assessment: While this transfer successfully fixes Germany, it creates a worse problem at Asia. Combined average fulfillment actually drops from ~66.1% (both baseline) to ~64.6% (transfer scenario). Consider a smaller transfer (20–25 workers) or pursuing the longer-lead net-new hiring process to avoid robbing Peter to pay Paul.
