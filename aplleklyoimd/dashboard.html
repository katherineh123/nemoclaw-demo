Assessment

⚠️ Trade-off Analysis: Transferring 50 workers from Asia to Germany achieves the goal for Germany — fulfillment rises above 70% by June 2026 and stabilizes at ~82% (🟡 At Risk zone, close to On Track). However, Asia collapses to ~47% fulfillment (🔴 deeply Critical), losing nearly 18 percentage points. This is a significant degradation that would likely trigger escalation protocols.

Recommendation: A transfer of 50 is too aggressive. Consider a smaller transfer (e.g., 20–25 workers) combined with net new hiring to balance both factories, or explore whether a phased transfer over several months could soften the blow to Asia.
