Executive Summary

Based on the most recent headcount composition (April 2026: 257 workers), the Germany factory is projected to deliver approximately 64.7% on-time fulfillment in May 2026.


This places the factory in the 🔴 Critical zone (below 70%). Even with cohort aging improving performance over the next 6 months, the model projects a plateau at ~67.3% — still below the 70% threshold.


Recommendation: Additional headcount is needed. At current staffing, the factory will not reach the 70% fulfillment target within the 6-month planning horizon. New tenured workers are required to close the gap.
