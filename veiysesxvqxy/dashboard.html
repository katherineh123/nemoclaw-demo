Analysis & Recommendation

🇩🇪 Germany gains: Fulfillment rises from 64.7% → 65.6% immediately (May), crosses 70% in June (70.9%), and plateaus at 82.2% by August once all transferred workers are fully ramped. This puts Germany in the 🟡 At Risk zone (acceptable) by June and approaches 🟢 On Track.
🌏 Asia collapses: Losing 50 tenured workers (27% of workforce) crashes Asia from 63.2% → 45.2%, settling at 47.0%. This is deeply in 🔴 Critical territory — a 18 percentage point drop. Asia was already below 70%; this makes it far worse.
⚖️ Net assessment: Transferring 50 fixes Germany but devastates Asia. You'd be trading one critical factory for another even more critical one. Consider instead:
  • Transfer 20–25 (smaller move): Gets Germany to ~70% by Jul/Aug while limiting Asia damage
  • Net-new hiring: Add headcount without reducing Asia — requires HR approval but avoids the tradeoff
  • Hybrid approach: Transfer 15–20 now + initiate net-new hiring process for both sites
