Key Takeaways

  • Germany clears 70% by June 2026 (month 2) and reaches 🟡 At-Risk / near 🟢 On-Track at ~82%.
  • Asia collapses to ~45–47% — well below the 70% critical threshold. This would trigger an escalation.
  • The transferred workers start as "new hires" in Germany (low initial productivity), so May still stays below 70%. The benefit kicks in from June onward.
  • Net effect: You fix one factory but break the other. Consider a smaller transfer (e.g., 20–30) or net-new hiring to avoid devastating Asia.
